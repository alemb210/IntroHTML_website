About Me

I currently work in IT at a law firm but I am hoping to start working in the field of software as I make the shift into college. At the moment, I am aiming to get a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from a university that is relatively close to home, such as Hunter College or SBU.

BTHS

The school I attend is Brooklyn Technical High School. I got accepted in March of 2019
with a ~530 SHSAT score. BTHS has a lot of flaws that come with a school of it's size,
but ultimately, I believe the major system makes it a very good school to attend if you
are already planning on working in a certain field. This year, I get to take classes such
as web development and database management, which I never would have expected
to take in high school.
