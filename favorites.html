Read about my favorite things:

When I am not at school or work, my favorite hobbies include listening to music, playing video games, and going to the gym. I've always enjoyed listening to music, but I started to branch out my tastes into alternative artists during quarantine. It's now my favorite form of art and a great way to get to know someone. I also enjoy playing video games, mainly with friends. I don't play as much as I used to, but currently I enjoy playing League of Legends and Fortnite. Working out is another hobby of mine, which I got into per a friend's recommendation. I enjoy working out a lot due to the physical and mental effects of exercise in general - it makes you feel a lot better! It's also another hobby that is very enjoyable with friends.

serval

My favorite animal is the Serval cat. These cats are native to Africa and roam savannahs and wetlands. These cats not only very cute, but very intelligent as well. While I found out about serval cats through domestic owners, it's not recommended to own them as pets due to their wild nature and our inability to properly care for them. However, there are servals who have been domesticated over generations and have owners that properly care for their needs, such as lots of exercise and whole prey meals.
